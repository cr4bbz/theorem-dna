# Glossary

## Claim

An interpreted unit of content extracted from a source.

## Formalization

A prover-specific representation of a claim.

## Theorem DNA

A layered identity object containing source, semantic, formal, proof, dependency,
environment, and ledger hashes.

## Ledger Event

An append-only event describing a change or verification result.

## Corollary

A derived claim accepted only after verification by a proof assistant.
